# Quick Start Guide

Follow these steps to complete your Zimbabwe e-Arrival Card.

## Before You Begin

Make sure you have the following information ready:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Personal Documents">
    - Valid passport (must be valid for at least 6 months beyond your travel date)
    - Current passport-sized photo (optional but recommended)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Travel Details">
    - Flight numbers or transportation details
    - Arrival date and planned departure date
    - Previous country of departure
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Accommodation Information">
    - Hotel/accommodation name and address
    - Contact phone number for your accommodation
    - City where you will be staying
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Emergency Contact">
    - Name of emergency contact
    - Phone number for emergency contact
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Step-by-Step Guide

### 1. Create Your Account

Visit [arrival.immigration.gov.zw](https://arrival.immigration.gov.zw) and click "Register" to create your account.

```
Email: your-email@example.com
Password: Use a strong password with at least 10 characters
```

<Warning>
Use a valid email address as your confirmation and QR code will be sent here.
</Warning>

### 2. Start New Arrival Card

After logging in, click **"Start New Arrival Card"** on your dashboard.

### 3. Complete Each Section

The form is divided into 7 sections:

| Section | Information Required |
|---------|---------------------|
| 1. Personal | Name, date of birth, nationality |
| 2. Passport | Passport number, issue/expiry dates |
| 3. Contact | Email, phone, emergency contact |
| 4. Travel | Purpose of visit, dates, flight details |
| 5. Accommodation | Hotel name, address, city |
| 6. Declarations | Customs and health information |
| 7. Review | Verify and submit |

### 4. Review and Submit

Before submitting:

<Check>Double-check all information matches your passport exactly</Check>
<Check>Verify your travel dates are correct</Check>
<Check>Confirm your accommodation details</Check>
<Check>Accept the declaration</Check>

### 5. Receive Your QR Code

After submission, you will receive:

1. **On-screen confirmation** with your reference number
2. **Email confirmation** with your QR code attached
3. **Dashboard access** to view and download your QR code

<Info>
Your QR code is valid for 12 months from the date of submission or until your arrival date, whichever comes first.
</Info>

## At the Border

When you arrive at a Zimbabwe port of entry:

1. Open your QR code on your phone or have the printed copy ready
2. Present to the immigration officer
3. Officer scans the code to retrieve your information
4. Answer any additional questions if asked
5. Receive your entry stamp

## Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I didn't receive my confirmation email">
    Check your spam/junk folder. If still not found, log into your dashboard to access your QR code directly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="I made a mistake on my submission">
    For minor errors, contact support. For significant changes (passport details, dates), you may need to submit a new card.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="My QR code won't scan">
    Increase screen brightness, clean your screen, or provide your reference number to the officer for manual lookup.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
