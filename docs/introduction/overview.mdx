# Zimbabwe e-Arrival Card

The Zimbabwe e-Arrival Card is a digital immigration form that streamlines the entry process for all visitors to Zimbabwe. This system replaces traditional paper forms with a modern, efficient digital solution.

## What is the e-Arrival Card?

The e-Arrival Card is an online form that collects essential information about travelers entering Zimbabwe, including:

- Personal identification details
- Passport information
- Travel itinerary
- Accommodation details
- Customs declarations
- Health declarations

## Who Needs to Complete an Arrival Card?

All foreign nationals entering Zimbabwe must complete an arrival card. This includes:

- **Tourists** - Visiting for leisure and sightseeing
- **Business Travelers** - Attending meetings, conferences, or business activities
- **Students** - Enrolled in educational institutions
- **Employment** - Working in Zimbabwe
- **Medical Visitors** - Seeking medical treatment
- **Transit Passengers** - Passing through Zimbabwe

<Note>
Zimbabwean citizens returning home are exempt from completing an arrival card.
</Note>

## Processing Times

| Submission Timing | Processing Time |
|-------------------|-----------------|
| 72+ hours before arrival | 24 hours |
| 24-72 hours before arrival | 48 hours |
| Less than 24 hours | May experience delays |

<Tip>
We recommend submitting your arrival card at least 72 hours before your planned entry date for the fastest processing.
</Tip>
